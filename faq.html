FAQs

How to register ?

Enter your basic information and Aadhar card for verification. Also enter a 6 digit Private PIN for your safety. And your account would be registered with us and we will provide you your account number.

How to Login ?

The account number provided and the Pin you set while creating your account. These two fields are required to log into your account.

What are the facilities provided to the user?

We provide a lot of facilities to the user with a user-friendly interface. Facilities like deposit money, withdraw money, transfer funds, change your account password and check your balance at any time, all at a single click.

How do I use deposit or withdraw money in the app?

After you enter the amount, you'll be provided with a unique reference number which is valid for 24 hours. Go to your nearest branch, present the reference number and get your work done without having to stand in queues.

What is the purpose of the reference number generated ?

The reference number generation eliminates standing in long queues for you to do your bank operations. With the help of a unique reference number you can now visit any branch, show your unique number and get your work done in no time.

What is the daily transaction limit ?

The daily transaction Default limit for the day is Rs. 5 Lakh. There is no maximum limit for Funds Transfer.

Are there any charges for fund transfer ?

No, fund tranfers to our bank accounts are absolutely free.

How much time does it take to transfer funds ?

The funds transferred to any MYRIS bank account instantly on a best effort basis

Can I transfer funds on holidays ?

Fund transfers are available 24x7 on internet banking Channels w.e.f. 16th December 2019 as per details given below.
  • From 8.00 AM to 6.30 PM – As per customer approval limit.
  • From 6.30 PM to 8.00 AM (including 2nd & 4th Saturday, Sunday & RTGS Holidays) – Less than INR 1 Crore (Transaction which are INR 1 Crore or above will be processed on the next RTGS day)
